About Me

My Name is Renata Wattier and I was born and raised in Brazil. I moved to United States in 2000 to attend Union College in Lincoln, NE. In 2005 I started working at State Farm in Lincoln, NE, and because of my job with State Farm I moved to Arizona in 2013. I am married to Jeff but you can call him Jeffy Pooh. I enjoy playing and watching sports, I used to play Roller Derby when I lived in Nebraska and had the opportunity to travel all over the country because of Roller Derby. If you have never attend a Roller Derby Bout and you enjoy watching full contact sports, you should give Roller Derby a try.

Jeff and I enjoy traveling and we go to one big overseas trip every year, we just came back from Brazil the Saturday before the boot camp started and we are planning a trip to Greece next year. We are also planning a trip to Germany during Oktoberfest in a couple of years. I am not quite sure what is more difficult, trying to find things to write about myself or compleing the assigment. If you are curious to learn a little more about me just ask!
